About WAYMOORE

WAYMOORE is a proudly African health, beauty, and home-care brand dedicated to promoting natural wellness and everyday living through safe, effective, and plant-based products.

Our mission is simple: to help individuals and families live healthier, more confident, and more vibrant lives using nature-powered solutions they can trust.

We believe that true wellness goes beyond what you eat or drink—it includes how you care for your body, your hair, your skin, and even your home. That is why our product line is intentionally diverse, covering internal wellness, personal care, beauty enhancement, and household essentials.

Every WAYMOORE product is thoughtfully crafted to deliver real results without relying on harsh chemicals. From our nutrient-rich plant-based protein mixes to our revitalizing hair care formulas, refreshing teas, feminine care products, and gentle home-care solutions, each item is created with quality ingredients and customer well-being at the center.

Our Philosophy

At WAYMOORE, we are guided by three core principles:

Nature First

We prioritize natural, plant-based ingredients known for their healing, nourishing, and protective properties.

Holistic Wellness

We design products that support the whole person—internally and externally.

Everyday Simplicity

Wellness should be simple, accessible, and a part of daily life. Our products make that possible.

Our Product Range

Our wide range of offerings includes:

  • Health & Wellness: Slim Tea, Man Power, Everywoman Combo, Infection Cleanser, and plant-based nutritional mixes.
  • Hair Care: Growth oils, conditioners, African black shampoo, and deep treatment masks designed for strong, healthy, natural hair.
  • Beauty & Body Care: Soaps, feminine wash, enhancement oils, shower gels, and moisturizing hand wash.
  • Household Essentials: Powerful, safe, plant-based dishwashing liquid and heavy-duty car wash solutions.

Each category is designed to complement the others, creating a seamless ecosystem of wellness for you, your family, and your home.

Our Commitment

  • Empowering customers with safe alternatives to chemical-based products
